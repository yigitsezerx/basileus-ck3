#   dominus_apply_ancestor_echo_effect based on the chosen
#   parent/ancestor's strongest skill axis (martial / diplomacy /
#   stewardship / intrigue / learning), with a generic fallback.
#   Duration is 10 years (matching dominus_forge_own_path).
# ============================================================
